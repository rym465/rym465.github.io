PASSPORTS

Assess your passport needs here.
You need aNew Passport IF
  • you have never had a passport
  • you have lost a valid passport
  • your passport was issued more than 15 years ago
  • your passport was issued prior to your 16th birthday
  • your passport is damaged or mutilated

You need aPassport Renewal IF
  • you are able to submit your most recent US passport
  • your most recent passport was issued in your current name
  • your most recent passport was issued within the past 15 years
  • your most recent passport was issued when you were 16 years of age or older
NOTE: If you LOST your passport, you must apply for a NEW passport

You need a Second Valid Passport IF
  • you have a valid passport with travel restrictions due to existing visas within the passport

You need a Name Change IF
  • you have experienced a qualifying name changing event (Marriage, Divorce, etc...)
